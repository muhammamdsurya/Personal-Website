Sport Center Web Application

Project information

  • Category: Web Application
  • Client: My Project
  • Project date: 01 March, 2023
  • Project URL: https://github.com/muhammamdsurya/Sport-Center

An online platform designed specifically to connect sports enthusiasts with fitness centers and sports facilities available. This website provides information related to the schedules, facilities, and training programs provided by registered fitness centers. In addition, the website helps sports enthusiasts to booking facilities or training programs online.
